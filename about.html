About WPVFD

Volunteer service with hometown accountability.

Wills Point Volunteer Fire Department is a volunteer department serving the Wills Point area from its station on North 5th Street and supporting local emergency response with a community-first mindset.

Who We Are

A local department built around people willing to serve.

Volunteer fire departments work because neighbors step forward. WPVFD reflects that model: local members supporting readiness, response, teamwork, and public trust for the people they serve every day.

This website positions the department like strong modern VFD websites do, with clear contact information, community resources, opportunities to help, and a straightforward explanation of the department's role.

Department Snapshot

  • Volunteer fire department serving Wills Point and Van Zandt County ESD No. 4.
  • Primary station located at 311 N 5th Street.
  • Non-emergency department line: (903) 873-3011.
  • Public updates are shared through WillsPointFire on Facebook.

What Matters Most

Readiness, service, and trust.

Readiness

Training, apparatus, gear, and operational coordination all support dependable emergency response.

Service

Responding when called and showing up for the community beyond the emergency scene.

Trust

Providing clear public information and maintaining a visible, useful presence for Wills Point residents.
