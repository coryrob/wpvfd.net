Wills Point Volunteer Fire Department

Serving our neighbors with emergency response, prevention, and preparedness. We are proudly 100% volunteer firefighters committed to protecting lives, property, and community spirit.

Become a Volunteer See Community Events

How We Serve

Our team responds around the clock and helps the community prepare before emergencies happen.

Emergency Fire Response

Rapid response to structure, grass, and vehicle fires with coordinated incident command and mutual aid support.

Rescue & Public Safety Support

Assistance at vehicle incidents, severe weather events, and community safety operations in partnership with local agencies.

Fire Prevention & Education

School visits, smoke alarm awareness, and practical fire safety guidance to reduce risk for homes and local businesses.

Community Standby

Visible support at town events and seasonal activities so families can gather with confidence and peace of mind.

Powered by Volunteers

Every call, every drill, and every public event is made possible by dedicated volunteers. From new recruits to experienced firefighters, our department depends on neighbors helping neighbors.

  • No prior fire experience required to start
  • Ongoing training and mentorship opportunities
  • Flexible ways to serve: operations, support, and outreach
