The Wills Point Volunteer Fire Department is an all-volunteer department serving the Wills Point area and Van Zandt County ESD No. 4 with emergency response, preparedness, and a strong local commitment to public safety.

What We Do

Response, readiness, and local public safety support.

WPVFD exists to protect life and property, assist during emergencies, and stay ready to respond when the community needs help. The department website should reflect that mission with a clear, practical structure residents can use immediately.
