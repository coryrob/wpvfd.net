Volunteer With WPVFD

There is more than one way to serve.

Many of the strongest volunteer department sites lead with recruitment because the department depends on local people stepping forward. If you want to help your community, contact the department to ask about current volunteer opportunities.

Operational Roles

Fireground, response, and readiness roles for people who want to train and serve directly.

Support Roles

Administrative, fundraising, logistics, and community-support work that keeps the department strong.

Local Impact

Volunteer service creates a visible difference in the safety and resilience of the Wills Point area.

Next Step

Contact the station to ask how you can get involved.

Support The Department

Community backing helps fund readiness.

Volunteer departments often rely on fundraising, donations, grants, and local support to strengthen equipment, training, outreach, and station needs. This site now gives that support a clear place in the public structure.

How To Help

  • Share department updates and community events.
  • Ask about current support or fundraising needs through the Contact page.
  • Follow the department on Facebook for announcements.
  • Encourage others to volunteer or support WPVFD locally.
